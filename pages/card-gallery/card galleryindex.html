GOHAN

is a half-breed Saiyan and one of the most prominent characters in the Dragon Ball series. He is the elder son of the series' primary protagonist Goku and his wife Chi-Chi, the older brother of Goten, the husband of Videl and father to Pan. He is named after Goku's adoptive grandfather, Gohan.

Design 2

SON GOKU

Goku, also known as Kakarot, is the main protagonist of the Dragon Ball series. Originally sent to Earth as an infant to destroy the planet, a head injury altered his programming. Instead of becoming a destructive warrior, Goku grew up pure-hearted, fighting for good. He embodies compassion, empathy, and unwavering bravery, making him one of Earth’s greatest defenders.

FRIEZA

Hails from the Frieza Race, known for their cruelty and power. His father, King Cold, recognized Frieza’s prodigious abilities from birth. Frieza’s overwhelming strength and genius for evil led Cold to hand control of the universe to him while he was still a child.

PICCOLO

The series follows the adventures of protagonist Son Goku from his childhood through adulthood as he trains in martial arts. He spends his childhood far from civilization until he meets a teen girl named Bulma, who encourages him to join her quest in exploring the world in search of the seven orbs known as the Dragon Balls, which summon a wish-granting dragon when gathered. Along his journey, Goku makes several other friends, becomes a family man, discovers his alien heritage, and battles a wide variety of villains, many of whom also seek the Dragon Balls.
